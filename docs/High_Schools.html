What do we see here?

Fairfax county is an unusual county surrounding Washington DC, Prince William County, and Loudon County. It varies in land use. All the way from Deciduous forests to Highly developed areas. There are many high schools encompassed in the Fairfax county boundary each school district having a unique make up of land uses.
